FAQs

We have top quality machines and weight sets from major industry brands such as Precor, Cybex and Hammer Strength! These machines are cleaned regularly and staff are on hand to advise in their safe usage.

We offer a wide range of class and trainer choices for people looking to build strength, improve fitness, and lose weight. Classes provide a fun social means of working out while our trainers can provide a gym experience tailored to your specific needs.

Currently we only offer a monthly subscription service. We plan to implement annual and pay as you go options in future. Make sure to avail of our current first month offer!

Allstar Gyms are open 364 days a year (closed on Christmas Day). Opening times are between 06.00 and 23.30 Monday to Friday and 06.30 to 21.00 Saturday, Sunday, and Bank Holidays.

Please bring your access card as our entrances are automated. Also, bring appropriate gym attire as well as a hand towel and water bottle. However, we have have vending machines that provide refreshments and clean towels just incase.
